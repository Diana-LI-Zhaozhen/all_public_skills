## IFRS 17 Profit Decomposition (Approximate)

```
Insurance Service Result (consolidated):
  Insurance Revenue:       570,717
  Insurance Service Exp:  (527,170)
  Net Reinsurance:           6,373  (= 35,309 - 28,936)
  Insurance Service Result:  49,920  (positive! ≠ typical life)

Investment Result:
  Investment Income:        44,936
  Fair Value Changes:       16,144
  Other Income:             31,851
  Impairments etc:          (1,175)
  Approx Investment Res:    91,756

Insurance Finance:
  Insurance Finance Loss:  (45,898)
  Reinsurance Finance:       1,111
  Net Insurance Finance:   (44,787)

Net Profit ≈ Service + Investment + Finance - Tax
  = 49,920 + 91,756 - 44,787 - 11,473 ≈ 85,416 (adjusting for eliminations)
```

**Key insight:** Unlike pure life insurers, PICC's insurance service result is POSITIVE (not negative) due to large P&C underwriting profit. The P&C segment's strong underwriting (COR 97.6%,承保利润124.43亿) offsets the life segment's negative service result under IFRS 17.

## Key Takeaways

1. **PICC is a composite insurer** — the P&C segment dominates (511,594M revenue vs life 25,337M)
2. **CSM growth is strong** at +13.3% (128,759 vs 113,688) — future profit trajectory positive
3. **New business CSM momentum** — PICC Life NBV +64.5%, new CSM +22.8%; PICC Health NBV +22.5%
4. **Investment assets grew 15.8%** to 1.90T, with total yield stable at 5.7%
5. **Investment income surged 78.7%** — partly a low-base effect from 2024 (25,139M was unusually low)
6. **P&C underwriting remains strong** — COR 97.6%,承保利润12.4B
7. **Premium income grew across all segments** — Life +18.9%, Health also positive
8. **Total assets** crossed 2.0T for the first time (2,027,683M)
