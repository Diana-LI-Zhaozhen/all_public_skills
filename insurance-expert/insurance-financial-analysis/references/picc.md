## Analytical Insights

### Business Mix Note
- PICC is predominantly a P&C insurer (人保财险). The P&C segment dominates consolidated figures.
- Life and health segments are growing rapidly but are smaller in scale.
- NBV growth in both life (+71.7%) and health (+51.0%) segments is exceptional.

### CSM Growth
- PICC Life CSM 92,555 (+22.4%) — strongest CSM growth among all A-share peers
- PICC Health CSM 20,189 — smaller but growing
- New business CSM 14,151 (+53.2%) — very strong new business contribution
