## Data Conventions

- **Chinese insurers (A+H)**: Primary data from CNInfo A-share annual reports (RMB). Supplementary data from H-share (HKEX) filings via yfinance (also RMB for Chinese companies).
- **HK/International insurers**: Primary data from HKEX annual reports / yfinance (HKD unless noted). AIA and Prudential group reporting in USD — HKD figures are for HKEX reference.
- **IFRS 17 transition**: Chinese insurers adopted IFRS 17 from FY2023. Hong Kong/International insurers adopted from FY2023 (AIA, Prudential from 2023; Manulife from 2023). **2021-2022 data is under IFRS 4 / old CAS — NOT directly comparable to 2023+.**
- **Blank = Unavailable**: Data not found in available sources. Marked as "—".
