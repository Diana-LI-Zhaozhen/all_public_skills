## Analytical Insights

### Insurance Manufacturing at HSBC
- Insurance is a small but growing component of HSBC's Wealth & Personal Banking segment
- Insurance Revenue growing rapidly: +21.8% (FY2024) → +17.3% (FY2025)
- Insurance Service Result expanding: +21.5% → +39.3%
- CSM growing steadily at ~8-9% CAGR from $1,024M (FY2022) to $1,312M (FY2025)

### CSM Sensitivities
- Negative CSM sensitivity to +1% yield (-$310M) — rate increases reduce CSM value
- Positive sensitivity to -1% yield (+$322M) — rate cuts increase CSM
- Equity sensitivity smaller (-$64M for +1%, -$75M for -10%)
- Credit spread sensitivity limited (-$15M to +$74M)
