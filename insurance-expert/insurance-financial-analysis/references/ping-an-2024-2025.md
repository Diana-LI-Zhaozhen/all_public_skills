## Analytical Insights

### Unique Traits vs Pure Life Insurers

**1. 营运利润 (Operating Profit) concept**
Ping An uses an operating-profit metric that strips out short-term investment volatility. For the life insurance business, it locks investment returns at 4.0% (the embedded value assumption). This means:
- Operating profit is **smoother** than net profit
- 2025 operating profit +10.3% vs net profit +6.5% — the net profit was actually more conservative!
- This contrasts sharply with China Life, whose profit swung +44% on investment gains
- Ping An's approach is deliberately conservative — it does not recognize equity market gains as operating income

**2. Composite group complexity**
- Ping An consolidated total assets of 13.9T include 3.4T of bank loans and 3.6T of customer deposits
- The insurance-specific investment portfolio is 6.49T (47% of group assets) — NOT the same as group total investment assets
- Banking segment generates 426B net profit but consumes significant capital (CET1 ratio 9.36%)
- For insurance-only comparisons, use the insurance segment disclosures, not group-level figures
- The group ROE of 14.0% is dragged down by banking (which requires high capital but generates lower ROE)

**3. CSM decline is a concern**
- Ping An CSM has declined for 2 consecutive years (768B → 731B → 725B)
- New business CSM (36.7B) + interest accretion (22.8B) = 59.5B, less than CSM amortization (68.2B)
- The gap of ~8.7B is filled by estimate changes (2.5B in 2025, was -25.4B in 2024 which was a big drain)
- This is an existential challenge for a life insurer — the "future profit reservoir" is shrinking
- China Life's CSM grew (+3.5%) — this is a meaningful advantage

### Profit Attribution

- **Insurance service result (寿险)**: ~84.0B positive ✅ — Ping An's life insurance underwriting is profitable, unlike China Life's negative service result
- **财产保险**: contributes ~16.9B operating profit, with COR 96.8% (underwriting profit)
- **投资 service result (寿险)**: 27.3B, +55.5% YoY — investment gains recognized in operating profit
- **银行业务**: 24.7B operating profit, stable
- **Other segments**: asset management -3.8B, fintech +0.2B, other -3.4B

Key insight: Ping An's **diversification works** — insurance (life + P&C) + banking + fintech provide multiple profit streams. The bank alone contributes ~18% of total operating profit.

### Investment Strategy Signals

- Aggressive equity allocation increase: 16.4% → 24.3% (+7.9pp), stock holdings +119% to 958B
- Bond allocation sharply reduced: 61.7% → 55.0% (-6.7pp), absolute bond holdings barely increased
- Real estate exposure stable at 2.2% (143.9B) — watch for property cycle risk
- 10-year average net yield 4.8% > current 3.7% — trend of declining reinvestment yields is real
- Uses innovative channels: gold pilot, private equity funds, infrastructure债权计划
