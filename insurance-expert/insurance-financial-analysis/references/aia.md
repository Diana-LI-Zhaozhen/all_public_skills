## Analytical Insights

### Market Position
- AIA is the largest pure life insurer in Asia by market capitalization
- Total assets US$345B — comparable to China Life (RMB 7.6T ≈ US$1.05T)
- CSM of US$64.9B is the largest CSM among all 11 tracked insurers (vs Prudential $25B, CPIC $49B equivalent)
- VONB of US$5.5B (+15%) indicates strong new business momentum

### CSM Analysis
- CSM US$64,945M (+15%) — the largest "future profit reservoir" in the peer group
- New business CSM US$9,110M (+17%) comfortably offsets CSM release of $6,224M
- CSM release-to-OPAT ratio: ~87% ($6,224M / $7,136M)
- CSM provides ~10+ years of profit runway

### Profit Quality
- 80% of operating profit from insurance services — high quality, underwriting-driven
- Investment return variances broadly neutral — profit not dependent on market timing
- OPAT +12% per share indicates shareholder value growth
