## Analytical Insights

### Profit Attribution
- 2025 profit growth (+44.1%) was almost entirely investment-driven
- 保险服务业绩 was NEGATIVE (‑65,112 in 2025, ‑28,204 in 2024) — underwriting alone loses money
- Investment income surged from 70,378 to 198,457 (+182%) reflecting realized gains on equity portfolio
- Comprehensive income (106,999) was much lower than net profit (156,552) due to large OCI losses (‑49,553) from bond yield changes

### Investment Strategy Signal
- Massive equity allocation increase (19.19% → 22.57%), stock holdings +66.7%
- Public market equity >1.2 trillion RMB, +450 billion YoY
- Clear policy response to "长期资金入市" (long-term capital entering markets)
- 浮动收益型业务 near 50% of new business — shifting rate risk to policyholders

### CSM Implications
- CSM grew only 3.5% vs profit +44.1% → profit growth is NOT from core insurance operations
- New business CSM declined 8.0% → low rates compressing new policy margins
- But CSM stock of 768B is still 3.6x annual insurance revenue → thick future profit buffer
- CSM amortization (~68.5B/year) provides ~11 years of steady profit release

### Risk Flags
- Net investment yield only 3.50% vs total yield 6.09% — 259bp gap is extreme, mostly from equity gains
- Three-year average yield 4.76% is more representative of sustainable return
- 承保财务损益 -258.9B largely offset by bond FV gains on the asset side — IFRS 17 accounting noise
- Effective tax rate jumped from 5.4% to 13.8% — check for one-off tax normalization
