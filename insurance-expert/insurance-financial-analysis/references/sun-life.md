## Key Takeaways

1. **CSM growth is healthy** — $14.5B from $13.4B (+8.4%), new business CSM $1.7B covers amortization
2. **Underlying ROE 18.2%** approaching medium-term 20% target
3. **Profit mix diversified**: insurance 57%, asset management 43%
4. **Capital very strong**: LICAT 157% significantly above OSFI minimums
5. **Asia is the growth engine**: segment assets +13.9%, underlying net income +19.3%
6. **Reported vs underlying gap widened**: market impacts ($381M) and intangible amortization ($215M) were the main reconciling items
7. **Investment portfolio remains conservative**: 73% fixed income (debt + mortgages), only 6% equities
8. **Unlike Chinese life insurers**: Sun Life's insurance service result is POSITIVE ($3.5B) — meaningful underwriting profit, not purely investment-driven
