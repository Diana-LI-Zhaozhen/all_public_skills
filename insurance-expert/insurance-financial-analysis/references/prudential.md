## Analytical Insights

### Profit Quality
- Insurance Service Result positive ($2,624M) — Prudential's underwriting is profitable
- Insurance revenue growing 3 years consecutively (+10.5%→+7.0%→+16.7%)
- Profit surged in FY2025 driven by investment gains + insurance finance unwind
- CSM flat at $25B between FY2024-FY2025 — the profit reservoir is stable

### CSM Analysis
- CSM $25B with annual amortization of ~$2.6B → ~10 years of profit runway
- New business CSM $2.77B covers amortization $2.55B
- Estimate changes of $2.04B provide additional flexibility
