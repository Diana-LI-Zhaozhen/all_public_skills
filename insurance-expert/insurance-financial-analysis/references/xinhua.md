## Analytical Insights

### Performance Highlights
- 2025 net profit record high (362.84亿, +38.3%)
- NBV surged 57.4% to 98.42亿 — significantly outpacing CPIC (40.1%)
- Investment yield 6.6% is the highest among the 3 A-share life insurers

### CSM & Profit Quality
- CSM grew 3.3% (1,817亿), new business CSM +8.0%
- Profit growth outpaced CSM growth significantly — investment-driven
- Net yield only 2.8%, the lowest — total yield 6.6% relies on realized gains
