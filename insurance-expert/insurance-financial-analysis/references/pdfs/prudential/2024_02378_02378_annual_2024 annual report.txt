Notification of publication of 2024 Annual Report and Notice of Annual General Meeting  
 
We are delighted to notify you that the annual report of Prudential plc (the “Company”) for the year ended 31 December 2024 (the “2024 Annual 
Report”) and the notice of annual general meeting of the Company to be held on 14 May 2025 (the “Current Corporate Communications”) in 
English and Chinese are available on the website of the Company at www.prudentialplc.com/en/news-and-insights/all-news/hkex/ (the 
“Company Website”) and the website of Hong Kong Exchanges and Clearing Limited at www.hkexnews.hk (the “HKEx Website”).  
 
If you would like to receive paper copies of the Current Corporate Communications and all future Corporate Communications2 of the Company, 
please provide reasonable notice to the Company: (i) by returning the completed and signed Request Form overleaf to the Company c/o its Hong 
Kong branch share registrar (the “Hong Kong Share Registrar”), Computershare Hong Kong Investor Services Limited, using the mailing label 
provided at the bottom of the Request Form (no postage stamp is required if mailed in Hong Kong); or (ii) by sending a scanned copy of the 
completed and signed Request Form by email to prudential.ecom@computershare.com.hk; or (iii) in writing by mail to the Hong Kong Share 
Registrar, Computershare Hong Kong Investor Services Limited at 17M Floor, Hopewell Centre, 183 Queen’s Road East, Wan Chai, Hong Kong. 
The Request Form may also be downloaded from the Company Website at www.prudentialplc.com/en/news-and-insights/all-news/hkex/ and 
the HKEx Website at www.hkexnews.hk. 
 
Should you have any queries relating to the above, please call the service hotline of the Hong Kong Share Registrar at (852) 2862 8688 during 
business hours (9.00am to 6.00pm, Monday to Friday, excluding Hong Kong public holidays).
